What is Multimodal AI?

Multimodal AI models process and generate data from multiple modalities, such as text, images, and audio.

Example: CLIP (Contrastive Language–Image Pretraining) learns joint representations of images and text for tasks like zero-shot classification.

Applications

  • Image captioning
  • Visual question answering
  • Text-to-image generation (e.g., DALL·E)
